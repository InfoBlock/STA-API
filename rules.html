§1.1 - English is the only language to be used in all channels.

§1.2 - Alt Accounts
Alt accounts are prohibited exceptions to this are made for VTC event or Management accounts that are being used in professional capacity. VTC poaching is not a professional capacity.

§1.3 - You cannot contact TruckersMP staff regarding issues or punishments issued by STA Management.

§1.4 - Appropriate behavior is required at all times. Zero tolerance for:

  • Homophobia
  • Ableism
  • NSFW content
  • Bullying
§1.5 - Staff must adhere to all rules at all times.

§2 Text channel policies

§2.0 - The channels should be used for their named purpose

§2.1 - Minimal swearing

§2.2 - English is the only allowed language in this server

§2.3 - Do not share any information that is personal to you

§2.4 - SFW memes must be used within this server

§2.5 - SFW Gifs must be used within this server

Rules

§3 Ticket policies

§3.0 - Tickets must be used in a professional way

§3.1 - A maximum of 5 tickets can be opened by 1 user at 1 time

§3.2 - Information must be in english

§3.3 - Tickets that are opened with wrong information are at the degradation of the staff whether it is closed or remains open.

§3.4 - Ghost tickets will be closed (If nothing is sent within the first hour of the opening time it will be considered a Ghost Ticket)

Rules

§4 Voice channel policies

§4.0 - No shouting.

§4.1 - Keep swearing to a minimum.

§4.2 - Keep background noise low.

§4.4 - Music must follow server rules, whether through bots or your microphone.

§4.5 - Ensure your microphone works properly if using voice channels.

Rules

§5 Media channel policies

§5.0 - Prohibited content includes anything offensive, pornographic, sexually explicit, drug-related, or criminal.

§5.1 - Sharing malicious software is forbidden.

§5.2 - Avoid sharing controversial subjects, political views, or religious opinions.

§5.3 - Share things that relates to games

§5.4 - Don't sent text in media channels unless a small caption

Rules

§6 Recruitment policies

§6.0 - Must be out of a previous VTC for more that 14 days/ 2 weeks

§6.1 - No more than 2 active bans

§6.2 - If joining from a blacklisted VTC or have been banned previous must appeal this before being able to apply to join Strategic Transports Alliance (even if the punishment wasn't directly issued to you are are technically banned by associating with the Blacklisted VTC)

§6.3 - If joining from a partner VTC you must wait 28->31 days depending on the month before being able to apply to join Strategic Transports Alliance

§6.4 - External staff can't be from a blacklisted VTC/group

§6.5 - Requirements are laid out for both Staff and drivers of which are shared with you when you open a application ticket

Rules

§7 Event invitation policies

§7.0 - An event page must be completed to the highest standard possible

§7.1 - All event invites must contain the following information
╔▷ Date:
╠▷ Server:
╠▷ Meeting Time: HH:MM (Timezone)
╠▷ Departure Time: HH:MM (Timezone)
╠▷ Starting Location:
╠▷ Ending Location:
╠▷ DLCs:
╠▷ TruckersMP Event Page:
╠▷ Slot Booking:
╚▷ Timezone: (If your VTC operates in a timezone other than UTC or BST)

§7.2 - A invite with Event Server on when the event is out of TruckersMP period of time to get a event server will get auto denied due to incorrect information

§7.3 - Event Priority
╔▶ Strategic Transports Alliance
╠▶ Partners
╠▶ Charity Convoys
╠▶ Other VTCs
╚▶TruckersMP, Sim Radio convoys (This is due to the levels of lag at these events)

§7.4 - For convoys scheduled across multiple months, please ensure that each convoy is submitted as a separate ticket. If multiple convoys are submitted within a single ticket, only the first convoy listed will be processed, and all others will be denied.

§7.5 - Strategic Transports Alliance reserves 3 days per convoy this is to provide our staff with admin time to set up and participate in the convoys

§7.6 - Strategic Transports Alliance hosts its convoys every 2 weeks on a wednesday starting February 2025 (this might change for 2026)

§7.7 - Strategic Transports Alliance does not attend convoys that are not related to trucks. Convoys/Events that involve Buses or cars will be denied

§10 Providing service

§10.0 - If either a CC or MT is providing STA a service they must sign a Agreement of Service

§10.1 - If STA or one of its sub companies is providing a service a service agreement will be provided for you to sign

This rule has been brought in due to recent incidents around receiving a service from a Team

Rules

§11 Punishments

§11.0 - Warnings - you can have 4 warnings logged on your account before a time out is issued

§11.1 - Timeouts - you can have 3 times outs logged on your account before a Kick is issued

§11.2 - Kick - you can have 2 kicks logged on your account before a Temporary Ban is issues

§11.3 - Temp bans - you can have 1 temp ban before a permanent ban is issues

§11.4 - Bans - you can have 1 ban before a blacklist is issued to you or your VTC

§11.5 - Blacklist - This is nearly irreversible action unless it is appealed directly to senior management.

§11.6 - Lower staff decisions can be overturned by higher staff or Senior Management.

You can send an appeal email to appeals@strategictransportsalliance.com or by making a modmail with the Strategic Transports Alliance bot and by submitting the Punishment Appeal form. All staffs are given punishment guidance for what punishments should be issued for rule violation.
